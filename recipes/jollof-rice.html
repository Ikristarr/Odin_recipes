Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof Rice is a popular west African Rice dish cooked in a rich tomato and pepper sauce

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Red bell pepper
  • Onion
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Blend the tomatoes, pepper and onions
  2. Cook the sauce with seasoning.
  3. Cook until the rice is soft and flavorful
