Lasagna

Description

This Lasagna is a rich and comforting pasta dish made with layers of sauce, cheese, and pasta.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the Sauce.
  2. Layer the noodles, Sauce, and Cheese.
  3. Bake until golden and bubbling
