Description

Fried Rice is a tasty rice dish cooked with vegetables, seasoning and sometimes meat or eggs.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Carrots
  • Green peas
  • Sweet Corn
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook the rice and set it aside
  2. Chop the vegetables.
  3. Fry the vegetables with seasoning
  4. Add the rice and stir everything together
